AUDIO PRODUCTION - "THE ORACLE"

During my multimedia studies, I developed a passion for audio production. One of my key projects was "The Oracle," a low-poly 3D first-person puzzle game created with Annelies Adamache, Damian Nowakowski, and Kiera Loughar-Clarke as our thesis project.

As the sound developer and composer, I crafted the game's audio landscape, including original music compositions and sound effects to enhance gameplay. I used the DAW Cubase for this project, and I learned how to use Audacity and Adobe Audition throughout my studies.

Working on "The Oracle" taught me the importance of collaboration and the role of audio in creating immersive experiences. This project refined my technical skills and deepened my understanding of how sound drives narrative. I am proud of this work and look forward to applying these insights to future projects.

VIDEO PRODUCTION

My deep interest in video production has led me to work on various projects during my studies, showcasing my skills in storytelling, visual design, and technical execution.

"NATURAL" MUSIC VIDEO

This is a music video I filmed and edited on Adobe Premiere Pro to the song "Natural" by Imagine Dragons. It explores a daughter's emotional journey as she escapes her troubled relationship with her father.

Originally, the plot centred on a man leaving a toxic relationship. However, an actor’s sudden leaving made a significant change to the story.

I adapted the storyline to focus on family and personal resilience, creating a raw and personal portrayal of the protagonist's journey. This adaptation created a captivating story that brings out the characters' emotional depth and complexity.

TIMELESS CLASSIC

This advertisement, created with Damian Nowakowski, Kiet Ngo, and Reoland Pepa, using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and After Effects, features Bonnie Tyler's song "Holding Out for a Hero." It highlights the timeless elegance and enduring appeal of Versace fragrance.

We designed this to showcase the perfume's lasting impact across different eras. We added comedic elements to keep it funny and relevant.

SERENIFY

Serenify is a compact, screenless wearable device designed to help stressed college students manage anxiety. Controlled via a smartphone app for iPhone and Android, it detects high heart rates and guides users through calming vibrational breathing patterns. This mock-up design combines wellness and technology, allowing users to monitor stress, engage in personalised mindfulness practices, and track mood for mental well-being.

Key Features:

  • Simple Setup: Streamlined for ease of use during stressful moments.
  • Unified System: Paired with a companion bracelet for holistic wellness management.
  • Minimalist Interface: Calming colour palette and simple design.
  • Mood Tracking: Calendar indicates mood patterns and stress factors.
  • Self-awareness: Profile catalogues mood logs and uses journaling for growth.
  • Community Support: Real-time conversations with professionals and peers.
  • Educational Content: Provides insights and coping strategies.
  • Biofeedback: Bracelet vibrates as a reminder to check in with oneself.

Creating Serenify taught me the true value of user-centric design, emphasising listening, adapting, and improving.



BUDGET BUDDIE

Budget Buddie is a mobile app designed to help users manage their finances better, which I did in collaboration with Kiera Lougar-Clarke, Reoland Pepa, and Jenny Nguyen. It assists users in creating a budget template, planning savings for short and long-term goals, and understanding banking terminology. The app is tailored for college students and anyone looking to improve their financial management skills.

Key Features:

  • User-Friendly Design: Intuitive navigation with clear instructions and prompts.
  • Clean Interface: Simple, easy-to-use interface with clear labels and buttons.
  • Financial Education: Provides a serene backdrop for learning financial concepts.
  • Mock Budgeting: Users can practice income distribution and receive feedback.
  • Gamified Learning: Engaging learning experience with levels and progress tracking.
  • Interactive Quizzes: Immediate feedback and real-life budgeting scenarios to reinforce learning.

In Budget Buddie, users transition smoothly from learning foundational terms via flashcards to applying this knowledge in quizzes. This progression deepens their understanding and makes financial education both effective and engaging.

These UX/UI design projects have been instrumental in shaping my approach to creating user-centered applications. Each project presented unique challenges and learning opportunities, allowing me to refine my skills and design philosophy.

CODING

I've developed four distinctive coding projects: two websites and two games. These projects highlight my versatility in both web development and game programming.

MY FIRST WEBTSITE

This is my first ever coding project that combines a bit about myself, my photography, and personal hobbies. The website features a modern minimalist design with clean lines, ample white space, and a cohesive colour scheme.

I used HTML5, CSS3 (including Flexbox and Grid), and JavaScript for interactive elements. Bootstrap was employed for a responsive, mobile-friendly design.

Key Features:

  • Optimised for all device sizes, from mobile phones to desktops.
  • Includes image sliders, hover effects, and smooth scrolling for enhanced user interaction.

User experience was a priority, with intuitive navigation, a clear menu, and an uncluttered layout for easy information access.

BOSNIA WEBSITE

Inspired by my Bosnian heritage, I created this website to share interesting facts, cultural insights, and travel tips about the county.

I used HTML5, CSS3 with custom animations, JavaScript for dynamic content, a Quiz Plugin, and a Flashcards Library for interactive features.

Key Features:

  • A quiz to challenge and debunk Bosnian stereotypes with accurate information.
  • A list of must-visit Bosnian places with descriptions and images to inspire travellers.
  • Flashcards of traditional Bosnian dishes with photos and descriptions.

User experience was a priority, with intuitive navigation, a clear menu, and an uncluttered layout for easy information access.

GUESS THE WORD GAME

This is a fun and interactive "Guess the Word" game that I developed. Players have limited guesses to guess the word based on the length of the word, choose their preferred colour scheme, and replay the game as many times as they like. It offers a clean, customisable, and engaging experience.

I used HTML5, CSS3 for themes, JavaScript for game logic and interactivity, and Local Storage to save preferences.

Key Features:

  • Players can choose from various colour schemes.
  • New words and hints are provided in each session.
  • Intuitive and easy-to-use interface.

The game prioritises user experience with a simple design. It provides immediate feedback on guesses, and endless replay ability with new words, making it engaging and interactive.

MARVEL QUIZ

As a Marvel fan, I created a fun and engaging quiz to test players' knowledge of the Marvel universe. The colour scheme is inspired by Marvel's iconic red, blue and gold.

I used HTML5, CSS3 for styling, and JavaScript for quiz logic and interactivity.

Key Features:

  • Marvel-themed colours for an immersive experience.
  • Multiple-choice questions with immediate feedback.
  • Score tracking for a fun challenge and replay ability.

The quiz is designed to be user-friendly and engaging. The interface is simple to navigate, allowing players to focus on answering questions and enjoying the experience. The immediate feedback on answers keeps the quiz dynamic and interactive.
